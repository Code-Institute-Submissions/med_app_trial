Med Manager

Designed to help you manage your medications with ease.

Let's Go!

Create a medication list

Med Manager allows you to create a list of your medications, so you will always have an uptodate list at hand for hospital and clinic visits.

Weekly calendar

View a list of your medications by day and time. You can also see when your prescription finishes, so you never run out of your important medications unexpectedly.

View current medications due

By clicking on the 'NOW' link, view which medications you are due to take at the current time, either morning, afternoon, evening or night.

Physicians

Encourage your patients to use Med Manager in order to improve adherence, improve treatment outcomes and obtain insight into patient behaviour.

Meeting patient needs

This application was designed with the needs of senior citizens and polypharmacy in mind.

My aim is to allow people to manage their own medication in order to maintain independence and self-care where possible without compromising on accuracy, adherence or patient safety.
