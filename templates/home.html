Med Manager

Let's Go!

Create a medication list

Med Manager allows you to create a list of your medications, so you will always have an uptodate list at hand for hospital and clinic visits.

Weekly calendar

View a list of your medications by day and time. You can also see when your prescription finishes, so you never run out of your important medications unexpectedly.

Using the 'current meds link, you can view exactly which medications you are due to take NOW.

Tell your health care provider that you are using the Med Manager app to help you both manage your medications with ease

Meeting patient needs

This application was designed with the needs of senior citizens and polypharmacy in mind.

My aim is to allow people to manage their own medication in order to maintain independence and self-care where possible without comprominsing on accuracy, adherence or patient safety.
